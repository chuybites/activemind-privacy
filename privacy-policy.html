Privacy Policy

ActiveMind · Last updated: April 2026

Overview

ActiveMind ("the App") is a personal wellness and activity companion. Your privacy matters deeply to us. This policy explains what data the App collects, how it's used, and your rights regarding that data.

The short version: Your data stays on your device. We don't collect, store, or sell your personal information.

Data that stays on your device

All of the following data is stored locally on your iPhone using on-device storage and is never transmitted to any server:

  • Your name and daily step goal (set during onboarding)
  • Step count and weekly activity data
  • Mood check-in history
  • Journal entries
  • Activity completion records and streak data
  • App preferences (theme, notification settings)

This data is never uploaded, synced, or shared with anyone. If you delete the App, all locally stored data is permanently removed.

Location data

ActiveMind requests access to your device's location to:

  • Show nearby places (parks, cafés, restaurants, etc.) on the Explore screen
  • Display local weather information on the Home screen
  • Provide location-relevant activity suggestions

Your location is used in real-time to query third-party services (Google Places API and Open-Meteo weather API) and is never stored, logged, or transmitted to our servers. Location access is optional — the App functions without it, but location-based features will be unavailable.

Health data

If you grant permission, ActiveMind reads step count, walking/running distance, and active calories from Apple HealthKit. This data is:

  • Used only to display your activity progress within the App
  • Never stored outside of Apple's HealthKit framework
  • Never transmitted to any server or third party
  • Never used for advertising or marketing purposes

Health data access is optional. You can revoke it at any time in Settings > Health > ActiveMind.

Third-party services

The App uses the following third-party services for specific features:

  • Google Places API — to discover nearby places. Your approximate location is sent to Google to retrieve place data. Google's privacy policy applies to this data.
  • Open-Meteo API — to display local weather. Your approximate coordinates are sent to retrieve weather data. Open-Meteo is a free, open-source weather API.

No personal identifiers (name, email, device ID) are sent to any third-party service.

Notifications

ActiveMind sends local notifications (check-in reminders) at times you can configure. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and do not involve any server or push notification service.

Analytics and advertising

ActiveMind does not:

  • Collect any analytics or usage data
  • Use any advertising frameworks
  • Track you across apps or websites
  • Sell, share, or monetize your data in any way

Children's privacy

ActiveMind does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. The App does not require an account, and no personal data is transmitted from the device.

Changes to this policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the updated version on this page with a new "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
